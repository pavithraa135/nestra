Frequently Asked Questions

🔹 How do I register on Nestra? ▼
Click on the Register link in the menu, fill in your full name, email, username, and password. Your account will be created instantly and you can start your roommate journey!
🔹 How does the roommate matching work? ▼
After completing the compatibility survey, Nestra's AI analyzes your preferences — sleep schedule, cleanliness, social style, diet, and more — to find your best possible roommate matches with a compatibility score.
🔹 I forgot my password. What should I do? ▼
Use the "Forgot Password" option on the Login page to reset your password via email. If you need additional help, contact our support team at support@nestra.com.
🔹 Can I edit my survey responses? ▼
Yes! Simply revisit the Survey page, update your answers, and save again. Your match results will be recalculated based on your updated preferences.
� Is my personal data safe? ▼
Absolutely. Nestra takes your privacy seriously. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
🔹 How do I use voice input in the survey? ▼
In the survey page, click the 🎙️ "Speak" button next to the preferences field. Make sure to allow microphone access. Speak clearly and your words will be transcribed automatically (Chrome recommended).
🔹 Is Nestra free to use? ▼
Yes, Nestra is currently completely free to use. We'll notify you of any changes to our pricing model well in advance.
