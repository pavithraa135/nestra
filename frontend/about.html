About Us

Nestra’s mission is to make co-living safe, intelligent, and empowering — starting with women-first shared spaces powered by AI. Our matching engine takes into account lifestyle habits, sleep cycles, and personal preferences to find your perfect roommate. Through a blend of technology and community spirit, we aim to create comfortable, compatible, and caring living experiences.
